Organizers in Bristol say the event builds on years of grassroots effort to bring neighbors together and support local causes.

"This is something our community has been asking for, and we're glad to finally see it come together," said Rachel Clark, volunteer coordinator in Bristol.

Last year's turnout topped 3630 attendees, and organizers say they are hoping to top that number this time with an expanded lineup of activities for families.

> "We're proud of what this means for Bristol and the people who live here." — Rachel Clark

Volunteers and sponsors interested in getting involved are encouraged to reach out to organizers ahead of the date.
