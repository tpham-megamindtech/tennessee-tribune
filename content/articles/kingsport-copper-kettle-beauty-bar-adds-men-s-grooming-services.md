The news comes as beauty businesses across Kingsport report steady demand from both longtime regulars and a growing number of first-time clients.

"It's a big step forward for Kingsport, and it wouldn't have happened without a lot of people pulling in the same direction," said Michael Robinson, master nail technician.

Staff say appointment requests have climbed roughly 39 percent since word first spread on social media. The business currently employs 5 licensed technicians and plans to add more positions if demand keeps pace.

> "It took teamwork, but we got there." — Michael Robinson

Those interested can book online or by phone, and walk-ins are welcome as availability allows.
