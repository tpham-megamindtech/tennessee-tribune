The update is expected to draw additional visitors to Pigeon Forge in the coming months, adding to what has already been a strong season for Tennessee tourism.

"It's a big step forward for Pigeon Forge, and it wouldn't have happened without a lot of people pulling in the same direction," said Andrew Harris, park ranger.

Visitor numbers in the area were already up an estimated 10 percent over last year, according to regional tourism data, and officials expect the trend to continue through the fall.

> "It took teamwork, but we got there." — Andrew Harris

Travelers are encouraged to check ahead for hours and any seasonal changes before planning a visit.
