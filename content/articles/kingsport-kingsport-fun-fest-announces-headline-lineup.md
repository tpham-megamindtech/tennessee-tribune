Organizers in Kingsport say the event builds on years of grassroots effort to bring neighbors together and support local causes.

"It's a big step forward for Kingsport, and it wouldn't have happened without a lot of people pulling in the same direction," said Andrew Robinson, nonprofit director.

Last year's turnout topped 3593 attendees, and organizers say they are hoping to top that number this time with an expanded lineup of activities for families.

> "It took teamwork, but we got there." — Andrew Robinson

Volunteers and sponsors interested in getting involved are encouraged to reach out to organizers ahead of the date.
