Organizers in Oak Ridge say the event builds on years of grassroots effort to bring neighbors together and support local causes.

"This is something our community has been asking for, and we're glad to finally see it come together," said Sarah Davis, event organizer in Oak Ridge.

Last year's turnout topped 3852 attendees, and organizers say they are hoping to top that number this time with an expanded lineup of activities for families.

> "We're proud of what this means for Oak Ridge and the people who live here." — Sarah Davis

Volunteers and sponsors interested in getting involved are encouraged to reach out to organizers ahead of the date.
