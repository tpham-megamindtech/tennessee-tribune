The update is expected to draw additional visitors to Pikeville in the coming months, adding to what has already been a strong season for Tennessee tourism.

"It's a big step forward for Pikeville, and it wouldn't have happened without a lot of people pulling in the same direction," said Matthew Lewis, tour guide.

Visitor numbers in the area were already up an estimated 16 percent over last year, according to regional tourism data, and officials expect the trend to continue through the fall.

> "It took teamwork, but we got there." — Matthew Lewis

Travelers are encouraged to check ahead for hours and any seasonal changes before planning a visit.
