The news comes as beauty businesses across Murfreesboro report steady demand from both longtime regulars and a growing number of first-time clients.

"We've put a lot of work into getting this right, and we think people are going to notice the difference," said James Harris, master nail technician in Murfreesboro.

Staff say appointment requests have climbed roughly 35 percent since word first spread on social media. The business currently employs 10 licensed technicians and plans to add more positions if demand keeps pace.

> "This is exactly the kind of progress our community deserves." — James Harris

Those interested can book online or by phone, and walk-ins are welcome as availability allows.
