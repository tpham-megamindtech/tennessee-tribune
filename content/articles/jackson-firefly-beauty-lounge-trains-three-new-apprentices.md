The news comes as beauty businesses across Jackson report steady demand from both longtime regulars and a growing number of first-time clients.

"This is something our community has been asking for, and we're glad to finally see it come together," said Robert Thompson, spa manager in Jackson.

Staff say appointment requests have climbed roughly 37 percent since word first spread on social media. The business currently employs 3 licensed technicians and plans to add more positions if demand keeps pace.

> "We're proud of what this means for Jackson and the people who live here." — Robert Thompson

Those interested can book online or by phone, and walk-ins are welcome as availability allows.
