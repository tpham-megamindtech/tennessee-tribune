The news comes as beauty businesses across Spring Hill report steady demand from both longtime regulars and a growing number of first-time clients.

"This is something our community has been asking for, and we're glad to finally see it come together," said Daniel Moore, spa manager in Spring Hill.

Staff say appointment requests have climbed roughly 19 percent since word first spread on social media. The business currently employs 6 licensed technicians and plans to add more positions if demand keeps pace.

> "We're proud of what this means for Spring Hill and the people who live here." — Daniel Moore

Those interested can book online or by phone, and walk-ins are welcome as availability allows.
