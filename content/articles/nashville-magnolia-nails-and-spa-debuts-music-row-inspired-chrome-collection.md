The news comes as beauty businesses across Nashville report steady demand from both longtime regulars and a growing number of first-time clients.

"It's a big step forward for Nashville, and it wouldn't have happened without a lot of people pulling in the same direction," said Brittany Taylor, salon owner.

Staff say appointment requests have climbed roughly 30 percent since word first spread on social media. The business currently employs 5 licensed technicians and plans to add more positions if demand keeps pace.

> "It took teamwork, but we got there." — Brittany Taylor

Those interested can book online or by phone, and walk-ins are welcome as availability allows.
