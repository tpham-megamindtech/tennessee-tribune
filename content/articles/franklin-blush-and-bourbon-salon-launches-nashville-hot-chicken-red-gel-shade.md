The news comes as beauty businesses across Franklin report steady demand from both longtime regulars and a growing number of first-time clients.

"It's a big step forward for Franklin, and it wouldn't have happened without a lot of people pulling in the same direction," said Maria Martin, beauty educator.

Staff say appointment requests have climbed roughly 36 percent since word first spread on social media. The business currently employs 11 licensed technicians and plans to add more positions if demand keeps pace.

> "It took teamwork, but we got there." — Maria Martin

Those interested can book online or by phone, and walk-ins are welcome as availability allows.
