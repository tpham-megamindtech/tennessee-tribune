The announcement adds to a string of recent developments for the Clarksville business community, which has seen steady growth over the past year.

"It's a big step forward for Clarksville, and it wouldn't have happened without a lot of people pulling in the same direction," said Brittany White, chief executive.

Company leaders point to Clarksville's growing workforce and lower cost of doing business compared with larger metro markets. The move is expected to create or support roughly 64 jobs in the region over the next two years.

> "It took teamwork, but we got there." — Brittany White

Local economic development officials say they will continue tracking the impact on jobs and tax revenue in the months ahead.
